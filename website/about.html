ABOUT ME

Hello World! I'm Xinkai Ge, a dynamic creator deeply immersed in the realms of animation, game design, and interactive media.

I have honed my skills through various work, including advertising design and project management for the gaming industry. My passion for games led me to collaborate on marketing and advertising projects for renowned gaming brands like NetEase Games, where I combined data analysis, user insights, and creative strategy to produce engaging, interactive advertising campaigns.
Whether it's designing immersive game interfaces or managing large-scale gaming projects, I'm driven by the desire to create captivating, interactive worlds that players can lose themselves in. Proudly based in the vibrant city of Shenyang, I'm excited to continue pushing the boundaries of game design and interactive digital media. Let's build the future of gaming together!
